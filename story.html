Story of EZ Game!

EZ is a genius boy who love to code. He also love logic and a little bit math.
One day, he entered a competition called National Logic Competition (NLC), a student competition held by Schematics, an annual event of Department of Informatics, Sepuluh Nopember Institute of Technology.
Having such a big self esteem, he was sure he could win the competition. He was so proud of himself.
He managed to enter the the semi final round easily. But, in this round, his self esteem was torn apart. He couldn't defeat the committee in a game. He tried so many times but he couldn't beat the committee even once.
At that time, he didn't think about winning anymore. He just wanted to defeat the committee. But it was impossible at that time.
Several months after the competition, he found out the clue to defeat the committee. But, rather than challenged the committee, he decided to make a game. He implemented his intelligence to the game.
So now, the game has the intelligence of EZ. Can you defeat the game? :)
