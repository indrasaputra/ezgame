How To Play EZ Game!

  • EZ has several numbers, from 1 to 9. The numbers are unique. It means there is no identical number.
  • The goal of the game is to get a combination of 3 numbers whose sum is equal to 15.
  • Players can collect more than three numbers. But, players are allowed to take just three of them to be combined.
  • There will be exactly two players. You and EZ. Each player takes turn one after another. You have the authorization to decide who will make the first move.
  • In each turn, player must take exactly one number and save it to his/her side. The taken number can't be taken anymore.
  • A game is played until there is no number left or the game has a winner.
  • The winner is the first player who gets the combination of 3 numbers whose sum is equal to 15.
  • If there is no number left and there is no player win the game, the game is tied.


Example:

Your numbers : 7 6 3 8
EZ numbers : 1 9 4 2
EZ win the game because EZ get a combination of 3 numbers (9,4,2) whose sum is equal to 15
